My full name is Kristian Firedi. I'm currently a Master's degree student, and a freelance designer and web developer. Besides my love for linguistics and languages in general, I also nurture my passion in the area of arts, social and natural sciences. Learning is so fun!

I started out making stuff for games in my teenage days, such as levels for WarCraft, Rise of Nations, and, most of all, Counter-Strike.

Since then I have expanded the scope of my design interests, and I'm constantly learning new and creative ways of expression through visual arts.
