I'm Kristian Firedi, a strong admirer of all things design-related, as well as art in general no matter the medium, with a passion for both science and the ever-improving tech it creates.

My start was the hobby of making levels, textures, and simple 3d models for various games, some of which have received prizes and/or players acclaim, which has since led me into graphic design and coding.

My current engagements include mobile app and web development, with graphic design sprinkled on the side, but I am constantly on the lookout for new things to learn, and ways to improve.
