My full name is Kristian Firedi. I'm currently a Master's degree student of Romanian language and Literature at the Faculty of Philosophy in Novi Sad. But besides my love for linguistics and languages in general, I also nurture my passion in the area of arts and graphic/web design.

I started out with photo manipulation at the age of 12 when my parents installed Photoshop CS2, and later on during my gaming days, with which I've spent many, many of my teen years, I started developing my own custom levels for games such as WarCraft, Rise of Nations and, most of all, Counter-Strike.

The freedom of expression and creativity these tools gave me are the reason why I continued to learn more about design, and the end to this road is nowhere to be seen in the horison. At least not anytime soon.
