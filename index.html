ABOUT

My full name is Kristian Firedi. I'm currently a Masters degree student of Romanian language and Literature at the Faculty of Philosophy in Novi Sad. But, beside my love for linguistics and languages in general, I also nurture my passion in the area of arts and graphic/web design.

I started out with photo manipulation at the age of 12, when my parents installed Photoshop CS2, and later on during my gaming days, with which I've spent many, many of my teen years, I started developing my own custom levels for games such as War Craft, Rise of Nations and, most of all, Counter Strike.

The freedom of expression and creativity these tools gave me are the reason why I continued to to learn more about design, and the end to this road is no where to be seen on the horison. At least not anytime soon.
